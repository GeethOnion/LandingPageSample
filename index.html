We offer 3 courses for pilots:

It is round 80 hours of study material for Pilots. Included are 10 Units of Vocabulary, Grammar, Listening, Reading, Speaking and a Test after each Unit.

1) ICAO Aviation English Complete Course

We understand that obtaining a proficient level of English is essential for your career as a pilot. We meet pilots every week who have lost great opportunities because they did not have the English proficiency certificate. We have a team of teachers who are native speakers, pilots and ex-examiners who prepared the best collection of material for your preparation for this very important test. Trust our quality. Our course material offers a very intensive program. We try cover as much technical vocabulary, grammar, comprehension and fluency as possible.

We understand that often your career depends on this test and so we always work in a focused, intense environment to ensure the best results for you.

Studying at home: The possibility of studying at home is perfect for flexible schedules. We also worked as pilots, so we know that a pilot's schedule is not routine like in other professions, making it harder to attend regular schools of english.
